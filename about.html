About the site

This website is all about the difference between manga, manhwa and manhua. It also contains various recommendation of such comic from the author and top 10 best manga, manhwa and manhua comic.

All recommendation are from the author's perspective and enjoyment and such the readers are not force to like the same manga, manhwa, and manhua the author recommends.
