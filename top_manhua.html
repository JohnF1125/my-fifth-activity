Top 10 Manhua

10. Song of the Lone March

Song of the Lone March is a series that merges drama and action to capture the attention of its fans all around the world. The manga centers around an escaped princess named Yongning, who survived a coup and is hungry for revenge.

9. 1/2 Prince

The 1/2 Prince is a 16 volume series with a delightfully fun concept. In order to prove to her brother that she was the best gamer, the young girl created a male avatar in the same game that he was currently playing.

8. Once Again

For fans of manga that pulls at the heartstrings, there is Once Again. The story stars Yuan Ge, a man who has been living married in an unhappy relationship for quite some time. This all changes when he is suddenly informed that his wife has died.

7. Lovely Everywhere

Lovely Everywhere is a three-volume series that is the perfect starting point for fans of shoujo manga. Tong Danwei, like most any teenage girl, is obsessed with the idea of finding a boyfriend and getting married.

6. Martial Universe

Martial Universe leans a bit more into traditional shonen territory and has a heavy focus on martial arts. The story starts when Lin Dong gets gifted the power of cultivation and begins journeying across the countryside.

5. The One

The One is a series that focuses in on the fashion industry and a model named Cane Lele. Cane Lele stumbles into the industry after being coaxed by her aunt to try her hand at the fashion industry that her parents once loved so dearly.

4. Doulou Dalu

Doulou Dalu is an incredibly popular series that has even spawned sequels. This series is an isekai that follows a master weapon maker as he is reborn in a fantasy world and given a chance at a less stressful life.

3. The King's Avatar

The King's Avatar is another series that is focused on the world of online gaming. Ye Xiu is known as the battle god in the popular game Glory, but after years of success, he has decided to retire from the game.

2. The Legend Of The Sun Knight

The Legend of the Sun Knight is another great starting point as it is a short series with only five volumes. For fans of comedy and fantasy series, the main character, the Sun Knight, will bring leaders a lot of laughs.

1. The Ravages Of Time

The Ravages of Time is perfect for readers who enjoy military titles with some historical information sprinkled in. The series is essentially an adaptation of The Three Kingdoms, a story that focuses on three very different but successful families.
